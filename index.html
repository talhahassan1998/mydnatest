myDNA Tutorials

The existing myDNA workflow has been updated, and a more patient-centric approach has been incorporated. Now a doctor can recommend a myDNA test to the patient by clicking on the myDNA button available on the Patient Consult-Medication screen.

It will generate an SMS to the patient (on the patient’s registered mobile number with a practice) containing a link to the myDNA website with a registration code. Clicking on the myDNA website link will redirect the patient to the myDNA kit purchase page, from where the patient will be able to purchase the myDNA kit. Furthermore, the registration number received as part of the SMS will be used by the patient to register the kit once they have received the physical myDNA kit in their mail.
